Before my trip I made sure to plan out the trip to have a good idea of how long the trip would be, an estimated cost and to find cool locations to visit on the way. Check out the [world map](/world-map) for an interactive map.

As you can see, the route is pretty long. I also made a spreadsheet with some places to visit and popular tourist spots, as well as looking up required documents. One of the documents I need is the [Carnet de Passage](https://carnetdepassage.org/), which is a temporary vehicle import.

{{<image src="trip-preperation.webp" srcset=false alt="Carnet de passage import document and a world map showing the intended route">}}

On top of that I also had to apply for the universities, which had me fill out a ton of documents. I applied for [Aizu University](https://u-aizu.ac.jp/en/) first and got to visit the university on my vacation in Japan. For Japanese universities it is required to send the documents by letter, but I instead handed them in when I visited. I am also planning on applying for [Osaka University](https://www.ist.osaka-u.ac.jp/), however, they haven't opened for applications as of writing this.

Something I will also have to do before my trip is prepare my motorcycle. I have to fix the brakes and do some minor repairs, I will also have a mechanic look it over just to be sure it makes it as far as possible before I inevitably have to repair it on the road. I also have to affix my bags and pack my apartment into them, which will surely work out great. As of March 2025 I will no longer have an apartment in Denmark, so I have to bring as the things I need and only store a few things (such as my books, PC and clothes) at my parent's place until I can bring it to Japan.
