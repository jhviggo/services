My name is Viggo Petersen, I'm adventurous AF and love to travel. I'm going to Japan to begin my Master's degree in Software Engineering, but instead of flying there I figured driving there on my motorcycle would be a lot more fun. On top of that I started this blog and my [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@ViggosDetour) channel in order to document my trip and hopefully inspire more people to go on similar adventures!
